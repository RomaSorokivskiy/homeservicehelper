# Product Context — Our Home

## Product promise

Our Home is a private, local-first household operating system for two people. It should replace daily visits to separate service dashboards with one calm, fast command surface while leaving each specialist service as the source of truth.

## People and jobs

- Two residents with equal access and separate underlying service accounts.
- Daily jobs: see what needs attention, add/finish chores, manage groceries, choose dinner, find household items, start movie night, run apartment scenes, and understand whether the home server is healthy.
- Exceptional jobs: manage warranties, backups, incidents, accounts, and advanced service settings.

## Product principles

1. One intent, one action, no admin-panel hopping.
2. Calm by default; attention is earned by real exceptions.
3. Local-first, private, and useful during an internet outage.
4. Specialist services retain ownership of their data.
5. Password contents never enter the dashboard.
6. Desktop, phone, wall tablet, and TV remote are first-class inputs.
7. Motion communicates hierarchy and state; it never blocks an action.
8. Every release updates the English README, real screenshots, and measured quality metrics.

## Current sources of truth

- Vikunja: tasks and recurring chores.
- Mealie: recipes, meal plan, and groceries.
- Homebox: belongings, locations, warranties, and maintenance.
- Vaultwarden: credentials; dashboard exposes status and safe deep links only.
- Uptime Kuma: service availability.
- Future: Jellyfin for cinema and Home Assistant for apartment state/scenes.

## Success definition

At least 80% of routine household actions can be completed from the dashboard in under three interactions, with no secret exposed to the browser and no dependency on public cloud access.
